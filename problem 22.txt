 LED Toggle on Rising Edge
Design a circuit where a LED toggles on every rising edge of the clock.

Requirements

Module: led_toggle
Inputs: clk
Outputs:
led — toggles state on every posedge clk
Initialize led to 0 at time 0.
code:
module led_toggle (
    input  clk,
    output reg led
);
    initial led = 1'b0;
    always@(posedge clk)
    begin
        led <= ~ led;
    end
